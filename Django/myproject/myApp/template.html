Amendments to Schedule 3 of the Framework Agreement

Z1.1 Definitions

  1. The Indices Date means:
  2. where the Works Order Construction Period has an odd number of days, the day which is the mid-point of the Works Order Construction Period; or
  3. where the Works Order Construction Period has an even number of days, the first day of the second half of the Works Order Construction Period.
  4. The Adjustment Date means the date which is 3 calendar months after the Indices Date.
  5. The Contract Date Index is the CPIH index value on the Contract Date.
  6. The Adjustment Date Index is the CPIH index value on the Indices Date or, where the index value is not available for the Indices Date, the latest available prior CPIH index value.
  7. The Works Order Construction Period means the period between the starting date and the completion date for the whole of the works inclusive.
  8. The Initial Total of the Prices means the total of the Prices on the Contract Date excluding any subsequent adjustments made to the total of the Prices.
  9. CPIH is the Consumer Prices Index including owner occupiers’ housing costs published by the Office for National Statistics (or any successor index).

Z1.2 Price Adjustment

A compensation event occurs on the Adjustment Date, pursuant to which a change to the Prices pursuant to Clause 63.1 shall be calculated as follows:

Inflation Adjustment = ((Initial Total of the Prices / Contract Date Index) * Adjustment Date Index) - Initial Total of the Prices

The Inflation Adjustment shall be assessed in the form of a change to the Activity Schedule and shall:

  • (where Main Option A applies) be due on the assessment date following the Adjustment Date; or
  • (where Main Option C applies) be added to the total of the Prices.

Z1.3 Payment on Termination

Where Main Option A applies and either Party terminates prior to Completion but after the Adjustment Date, the amount due to the Contractor pursuant to Clause Z1.2 shall be revised as follows:

  • the Indices Date shall be revised to be the day which is the mid-point of the period between the starting date and the date on which a termination certificate is issued in accordance with Clause 90.1; and
  • the Inflation Adjustment shall be adjusted to take account of the revised Indices Date and any additional sums due to the Contractor or sums due to the Client as a consequence of this revision of the Indices Date shall be payable pursuant to clause 93.1 as an additional bullet point constituting an additional part of A1.

Z.2 Forecast Cap

In this clause, Z.2, Forecast Cap means [£x].([x] pounds only) and represents the Client's available budget for all of the works.

If the Project Manager instructs, the Forecast Cap as defined in this clause Z.2, shall be amended.

The Contractor shall confirm to the Project Manager as soon as it reasonably considers that the Prices may exceed the Forecast Cap, providing a document setting out why the Contractor considers the Forecast Cap will be exceeded and setting out any potential mitigations. The Project Manager may then issue an instruction changing the Scope. The Contractor shall not exceed the Forecast Cap and the amount due to the Contractor pursuant to Clause 51 shall not exceed the Forecast Cap without prior instruction from the Project Manager.
